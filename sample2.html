Mobile Legends

Mobile Legends (MLBB) is a 5v5, team-based online game. The goal is to destroy the enemy turrets, demolish their base, and achieve victory. You can prevent the enemy from destroying your turrets by attacking them and eliminating their players.

5 reasons why this game is my favorite:

  • I'm skilled at strategy games.
  • While there are toxic players, there are also many nice ones.
  • It is highly addictive.
  • I play it when I'm bored and done with my studies.
  • I played it frequently during the pandemic.
